    • Welcome!

    I am currently a Teaching Fellow at the University Paris Sorbonne. I recently received my Ph.D. at the Centre for Industrial Economics at Mines Paristech, PSL University in France. My research is on firm dynamics with a focus on innovative young firms and with a broader interest in competition dynamics. I investigate firm innovation strategies and how they are impacted by financial and environmental policies.

    I hold a B.Sc. in Operations Research: Financial Engineering from Columbia University, a Masters 1 in Quantitative Economics and Finance from Ecole Polytechnique and a Masters 2 in Analysis and Policy in Economics from the Paris School of Economics.

    Research interests: Innovation, Entrepreneurial Financing, Applied Microeconomics
